Snowboarding

One of my few hobbies includes snowboarding. I love to snowboard, and have snowboarded in 3 different continents so far. Hopefully, I can snowboard in all 7 one day. Here are some of the mountains I've been to.



My Favorite Mountains

Valle Nevada - Chile

The cool thing about snowboarding in the southern hemisphere is that it's cold in the summer time and hot in the winter time. Snowboarding in Valle Nevada was this first time I've been snowboarding in August. Hopefully I get to go back soon.

Garmisch-Partenkirchen

Garmisch is a mountain range in Germany, close to the city of Munich. I learned how to ski and snowboard here, and wish to go back and ride on the more advanced trails since I'm a bit older now. It's a great place to snowboard and learn about German culture.

Gore Mountain

Gore mountain is the highest peak in New York, it's about a 5 hour drive from New York City, and less than 100 miles from the Canadian border. I went snowboarding there for one weekend and it was great. Pack warm though, becuase its freezing cold. at the top of the mountain.
